SEVEN ISLANDS OF MUMBAI

Mahim

Mahim is a sought-after location for its beautiful sea views, connectivity to the city and amazing food joints.

Parel
Worli

Worli is among the most important financial hubs in the Mumbai. It also remains one of the most historic in the city.

Mazagaon

Mazgaon has a rich history,it influenced several communities, which makes it interesting.

Colaba

One of Mumbai's top attractions and the city's most famous monument, the iconic Gateway of India is a popular place to start exploring Colaba.

Little Colaba

The Old Woman's Island, also known as Little Colaba is one of the seven islands composing the city of Mumbai, India, and part of the historic Old Mumbai.

Bombay

Isle of Bombay was one of the Seven Islands of Bombay, an archipelago of islands that were, in the eighteenth century, connected to form the area of the modern city of Bombay in India.
